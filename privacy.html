Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026 extengr.com We respect your privacy
01
Introduction & Scope

ExtEngineer ("we", "us", "our") Pakistan mein operate karta hai. Ye Privacy Policy batati hai ke hum aapki information kaise collect, use, aur protect karte hain jab aap hamari website ya services use karte hain.

Is policy ka asar hota hai: website visitors, internship applicants, paid service clients, aur browser extension users sab par.

Agar aapko koi bhi privacy concern ho, aap seedha WhatsApp (+92 319 6977218) ya email ke zariye humse contact kar sakte hain. Hum 24 hours mein respond karte hain.

02
Information We Collect

Hum sirf wahi information collect karte hain jo service provide karne ke liye zaroori hai:

  • Contact Information: Naam, WhatsApp number, email address — jab aap contact karte hain ya apply karte hain
  • Internship Applications: Google Form ke zariye submit ki gayi information (naam, education, background, goals)
  • Payment Information: Transaction reference number — actual card/account details hum store nahi karte (ye EasyPaisa/JazzCash handle karte hain)
  • Project Requirements: Aapke project ya service request ki details jo aap WhatsApp ya form ke zariye share karte hain
  • Usage Data: Basic website analytics (page views, traffic source) — anonymized form mein
03
How We Use Your Information

Aapki information sirf in purposes ke liye use hoti hai:

  • Internship applications process karna aur selected candidates ko inform karna
  • Paid services deliver karna (resume review, mentorship sessions, LinkedIn optimization)
  • Custom development projects ke liye communication aur progress updates
  • Payment confirmation aur invoice generation
  • Service improvements ke liye feedback analysis

Hum aapki information kabhi sell, rent, ya unauthorized third parties ke saath share nahi karte.

04
Third-Party Services

Hum kuch third-party services use karte hain — unki apni privacy policies hain:

  • WhatsApp (Meta): Primary communication channel — Meta ki privacy policy apply hoti hai
  • Google Forms: Internship applications — Google ki privacy policy apply hoti hai
  • EasyPaisa / JazzCash: Payment processing — unki respective policies apply hoti hain
  • Google Meet: Mentorship video calls ke liye
  • GitHub: Code delivery aur collaboration ke liye

In platforms pe share ki gayi information unki apni privacy policies ke tahat aati hai. Hum recommend karte hain ke aap unhe bhi review karein.

05
Data Storage & Security

Aapka data secure rakhna hamari priority hai:

  • Client conversations WhatsApp pe stored hain (Meta's encryption ke saath)
  • Project files aur deliverables Google Drive ya GitHub pe securely stored hain
  • Payment records sirf transaction references hain — no sensitive financial data stored
  • Hum aapka data tab tak rakhte hain jab tak service active hai, phir delete kar dete hain

Koi bhi data breach ya security concern ke case mein hum aapko immediately inform karenge.

06
Your Rights

Aapke paas ye rights hain:

  • Access: Aap request kar sakte hain ke hum ne aapke baare mein kya information store ki hai
  • Correction: Galat information correct karwane ka haq
  • Deletion: Aap apna data delete karwa sakte hain (service completion ke baad)
  • Opt-out: Marketing communications se opt-out karne ka haq
  • Portability: Apna data portable format mein receive karne ka haq

In rights ko exercise karne ke liye WhatsApp ya email pe humse contact karein.

07
Cookies & Tracking

Hamari website minimal tracking use karti hai:

  • Basic analytics (page visits, traffic sources) — koi personal data nahi
  • Dark mode preference browser's localStorage mein store hoti hai — sirf aapke device par
  • Hum advertising cookies ya third-party tracking pixels use nahi karte

Aap browser settings mein cookies disable kar sakte hain — website basic functionality ke saath kaam karta rahega.

08
Changes to This Policy

Ye policy update ho sakti hai. Major changes ke case mein hum:

  • Website pe updated date post karenge
  • Active clients ko WhatsApp pe inform karenge
  • Previous version archive karenge

Continued use of our services means acceptance of the updated policy.
