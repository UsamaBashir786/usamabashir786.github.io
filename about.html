// my story

How it started

Main ek self-taught developer hun jo Pakistan mein kaam karta hai. Mera safar tab shuru hua jab mujhe pata chala ke browser extensions — wo choti si cheezein jo Chrome mein install hoti hain — kitni powerful ho sakti hain. Ek extension developer ke liye ek poora product ban sakti hai.

Shuru mein mujhe bhi wahi mushkilaat aain jo har naye developer ko aati hain: tutorials achhe nahi milte, mentor nahi hota, aur real projects pe kaam karne ka moqa nahi milta. Main chahta tha ke Pakistan mein seekhne walon ko wo guidance mile jo mujhe nahi mili.

Isliye ExtEngineer banaya — ek jagah jahan free internship, paid mentorship, aur real client projects sab available hon. Students ko sirf seekhna nahi chahiye — unhe kaam karna chahiye. Real projects, real commits, real experience.

Aaj main browser extensions, web apps, aur landing pages build karta hun — clients ke liye bhi aur apne students ke liye bhi. Har project ek learning opportunity hai, aur har student ek investment hai Pakistan ke future tech ecosystem mein.

// what I believe in

My values as a developer & mentor

Real Learning Over Certificates
Mujhe nahi lagta ke ek certificate aapko developer banata hai. Real projects pe kaam karna aur problems solve karna — yahi asli seekhna hai. Internship mein main isi cheez pe focus karta hun.
Transparency First
Koi hidden fees, koi false promises nahi. Jo price hai woh clearly batata hun. Payment pehle hota hai kyunke yahi professional way hai — dono sides ke liye.
Community Over Competition
Pakistan mein developers ki community grow karni chahiye. Isliye free internship deta hun — taake zyada log tech mein aa sakein, sirf woh nahi jo afford kar sakein.
Ship Real Work
Tutorial hell se niklo. Hum real deployed projects banate hain — cheezein jo actually live hain, real users hain, aur aapke GitHub pe real commits hain. Portfolio mein fakeness nahi chalti.
